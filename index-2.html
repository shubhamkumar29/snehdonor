Campaigns

Sponsor health of an unpriveleged Elder

SNEH needs precious funds and resources to enable the organization to empower the elderly and run its campaigns. We need funds for the following activities: • Eldercare treatment centers • Primary health care in villages • Health through sanitation awareness • Active social centers The funded programs strive to meet elderly care objectives. The initial startup infrastructure will be funded by SNEH and eventually the useful work done by senior citizens will become self funding. The care centers will be equipped with medical equipment and physiotherapy machines. Token amounts will be charged from non members to contribute towards the cost of smoothly running our centers.
